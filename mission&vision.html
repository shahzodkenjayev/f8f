Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

The mission of the Feed&Food Trading
Providing a function as a reliable and flexible supplier of food ingredients for human and animal nutrition.

The vision of the Feed&Food Trading
We are a leading company in our industry with a unique product portfolio & service. By operating innovatively and proactively in the food & feed industry, we are always able to grow.

 

Our 4 Core Values

Respect
We are proud of our company and respect our colleagues, customers, suppliers and other stakeholders.

Quality
We give the utmost care and attention to provide the best quality and service at any time.

Integrity
With our people and companies we do business on the basis of trust.

Responsibility
We foster ownership and responsibility of our employees.

Part of

The F&F Group is a flexible commercial supply company for liquid and dry dairy and vegetable ingredients in the international food and feed industry.
A union of strong and dynamic, international orientated companies in the ingredients market.  As part of the F&F Group, our companies Feed & Food Trading B.V., Fabelco B.V., Feed & Food GmbH, Nöll & Co. GmbH and F & F International B.V. profit from the extensive knowledge and experience of their subsidiaries and offer a high quality standard. Both in products as well as our way of working.
